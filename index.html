The 7-Month Battle Plan

March → October 2026  ·  Each month has a clear theme, tasks, and a milestone to hit

M1
March 2026 · Foundation
Learn the Language of AI Products
You cannot manage what you don't understand. This month is about building enough AI literacy to speak confidently in interviews and with engineers — without needing to code.
○
Complete "AI for Everyone" by Andrew Ng (Coursera — free audit). 6 hours total. Do it in 2 weeks.
○
Read "Inspired" by Marty Cagan — the PM bible. Chapters 1–15 first, rest next month.
○
Study 10 real AI product teardowns: ChatGPT, Google Bard, Notion AI, Grammarly, Zomato AI, Swiggy feed ranking, Freshdesk AI, Intercom Fin, Zendesk AI, Leena.ai
○
Start a "Product Thinking Journal" — every day, pick one app you use and write 5 lines on how AI makes it better or worse
○
Create LinkedIn profile: headline = "Customer Experience Professional | Transitioning to AI Product Management" — start following 50 AI PMs
✦ AI for Everyone — Coursera (free audit) ✦ Lenny's Newsletter (free tier) ✦ Product School YouTube
🏁 Milestone: Can explain what RAG, LLM, and AI agents mean in plain English to a non-tech person
M2
April 2026 · PM Fundamentals
Master the Core PM Toolkit
Learn how product managers actually think and work — frameworks, prioritization, roadmaps, metrics, and how to write a PRD. This is table-stakes for any PM interview.
○
Learn core PM frameworks: CIRCLES method, RICE scoring, Jobs-to-be-Done, North Star metric, OKRs. Don't just read — apply each to a BPO product scenario
○
Write 2 mock PRDs (Product Requirement Docs) — one for "Add AI chat to a BPO ticketing system" and one you design yourself. Post both on LinkedIn.
○
Take Anthropic Academy: "AI Fluency (4D Framework)" — free, certificate included. Directly relevant to PM interviews.
○
Learn basic SQL: SELECT, WHERE, JOIN, GROUP BY. Use Mode Analytics or SQLZoo (free). You'll need this for data analysis in PM role.
○
Attend 2 online product management events/webinars. Comment on 5 posts by AI PMs daily.
✦ SQLZoo (free) ✦ Anthropic Academy — AI Fluency (free) ✦ Reforge Blog (free articles)
🏁 Milestone: 2 PRDs written and posted. Can explain RICE prioritization in an interview answer.
M3
May 2026 · AI Depth + Anthropic Certs
Go Deep on LLMs & AI Products
This is where your BPO background pays off. Focus on AI applied to customer experience — chatbots, AI agents, contact center automation. You have domain expertise here that most PM candidates don't.
○
Complete Anthropic Academy: "Building with the Claude API" (8hr, free cert) — even though you won't code, understanding what engineers are building is essential
○
Complete Anthropic Academy: "Introduction to MCP" — MCP is appearing in AI PM job descriptions now. Get the cert.
○
Deep dive into AI in CX/BPO: Study Intercom Fin, Zendesk AI, Freshdesk Freddy, NICE CXone, Genesys AI. For each: what problem does it solve, how does it work, what's its metric of success?
○
Learn about prompt engineering basics (not coding — the strategy of designing AI interactions). Complete DeepLearning.AI "ChatGPT Prompt Engineering for Developers" (free, 1hr)
○
Write a 1,000-word case study: "How I would redesign [your BPO company's] support system using AI" — this becomes a portfolio piece
✦ Anthropic Academy (anthropic.skilljar.com) ✦ DeepLearning.AI short courses (free) ✦ a16z AI essays (free)
🏁 Milestone: 2 Anthropic certificates + 1 published case study on LinkedIn. 500+ profile views.
M4
June 2026 · Portfolio Building
Build Proof of Work
No-code tools mean you can build AI product prototypes without writing code. Build things you can show in interviews. Hiring managers want to see that you've shipped, not just studied.
○
Build a no-code AI prototype using Voiceflow or Botpress — a customer support AI agent for a fictional company. Record a 3-min walkthrough video, post it on LinkedIn.
○
Create a full product roadmap in Notion or Linear for "AI features for a mid-size BPO company" — include discovery, prioritization, metrics, and success criteria
○
Write 3 blog posts on Medium or LinkedIn: (1) "What BPO taught me about AI products" (2) "How Intercom Fin changed customer support" (3) "5 metrics I'd use to measure AI chatbot success"
○
Apply for 5 informational interviews — reach out to 10 AI PMs on LinkedIn asking for a 20-minute call. Not job applications. Learning conversations.
○
Complete a product analytics course: Amplitude's free academy or Mixpanel's free course. Understand retention, funnels, DAU/MAU.
✦ Voiceflow (free tier) ✦ Notion (free) ✦ Amplitude Academy (free)
🏁 Milestone: 1 working AI prototype + 1 product roadmap doc + 3 published articles. Portfolio ready.
M5
July 2026 · Interview Prep
Train for the AI PM Interview
AI PM interviews test 4 things: product sense, analytical thinking, AI knowledge, and communication. Each requires specific practice. Start applying now — rejections are data.
○
Practice 20 product case questions aloud: "Design an AI feature for Zomato", "How would you measure success of a chatbot", "What AI would you add to an HR system?" Record yourself.
○
Prepare STAR-format answers for: "Why PM?", "Tell me about a time you handled a difficult customer", "How do you prioritize features?", "What AI product excites you most?"
○
Start sending 10 job applications per week — target: Associate PM, AI Business Analyst, Product Analyst, CX Technology PM at AI startups, BPO tech companies, SaaS CX platforms
○
Cold email 3 hiring managers per week with a specific, short message + link to your best portfolio piece
○
Join Product Management communities: PM School India, Product folks India Discord, Reforge community (free tier)
✦ Exponent PM Interview prep (free videos) ✦ PM School India ✦ Naukri / LinkedIn Jobs
🏁 Milestone: 40+ applications sent, 5+ recruiter screens, 2+ first-round interviews booked.
M6
August–September 2026 · Execution
Full Interview Sprint + Negotiate
By now you should be in active interview loops. Don't slow down learning — but your main job is now getting offers. Treat every interview as a reps session.
○
Send 15+ applications per week. Broaden your search: Product Analyst, AI Business Analyst, AI Implementation Specialist — these are stepping stones, not compromises
○
Get 2 mock interviews from peers or mentors. Use interviewing.io or Pramp for free mock rounds.
○
Complete Anthropic Academy: "Claude on AWS Bedrock" cert — shows cloud + AI stack knowledge in interviews
○
Update portfolio with any new projects, refine your LinkedIn "About" section to tell a crisp narrative: BPO → AI expertise → PM skills
○
Research salary ranges, know your walk-away number, and prepare your negotiation script before any offer comes
✦ Pramp (free mock interviews) ✦ Anthropic Academy — AWS Bedrock cert ✦ Glassdoor salary data
🏁 Milestone: 100+ total applications, 10+ interviews, 2+ final rounds, 1 offer in hand.

Where & How to Find the Job
Your 5-channel job hunting strategy — don't rely on just one
01
Target BPO-Tech Companies First
Companies like iEnergizer, EXL, WNS, Mphasis, Teleperformance India are actively building AI into their CX products. Your BPO experience + AI knowledge = immediate credibility. These are your easiest entry doors.
02
CX SaaS Startups (Best Bet)
Freshworks, Kapture CRM, Leena.ai, Yellow.ai, Observe.ai, Convin.ai — all hiring PMs who understand support operations AND AI. Search "CX AI product" on LinkedIn Jobs daily. Set alerts.
03
Internal Transfer Is Fastest
Does your current BPO company have a technology or product team? Transferring internally to an "AI Transformation" or "Digital PM" role is often 5× easier than applying externally. Talk to your manager. Plant the seed now.
04
LinkedIn Cold Outreach
Identify 100 AI PMs at target companies. Send 5 connection requests/day with a specific message: "I'm transitioning from BPO to AI PM — I wrote this case study on AI in customer support. Would love 15 min of your perspective."
05
Titles to Apply For (Not Just "AI PM")
Associate Product Manager · AI Business Analyst · Product Analyst · CX Technology PM · AI Implementation Specialist · Digital Transformation Analyst · Conversational AI Product Lead · Customer AI Specialist
06
Your Anthropic Certs as Conversation Starters
Add all Anthropic certs to LinkedIn. They're specific, verifiable, and signal you've done the real work. Many recruiters are Googling "Anthropic certification" right now. Stand out.
